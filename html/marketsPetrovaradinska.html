About Petrovaradniska market

By the decision of the Assembly of the City of Novi Sad dated March 25th, 2011, the Petrovaradinska market was entrusted to the management, arrangement and maintenance of the Novi Sad Farmers Market.

With the ceremonial opening on June 8th, 2011, the citizens of Petrovaradin received a modern market, which will satisfy their needs in the supply of food and non-food products with its capacity. The market has been completely reconstructed. On the plateau of about 1100 square meters, a new surface was installed, a new water and sewage infrastructure was built, a parking lot was built, the market was fenced, lit, new stalls were installed, and 3 shops were built.

The market now has 70 stalls, 4 refrigerated showcases for the sale of dairy products and poultry and 3 bars.
